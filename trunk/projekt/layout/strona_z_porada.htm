Jak otworzyć drzwi bez kluczyka??

Opis porady

Rats and other household pests seek places that fill their basic survival needs of food, water and shelter. Once they have established a place to live and breed, pests can be difficult to eradicate, and they can destroy property and spread disease. But using poisons and traps to kill rodents can cause unwanted smells. So the best way to control pests is to prevent them from moving into your home in the first place

Instrukcja

1. Hide your discarded food by keeping a tight lid on your outdoor trashcan. Empty your indoor trash daily. Don't leave food in pet bowls, especially moist food. Sweep up crumbs and store open food cartons in sealed containers. Be sure not to leave dirty dishes out overnight, and clean your kitchen after meals so there are no spills or dried food left to attract bugs or rodents. Dispose of fallen and rotting fruit in your yard. If you have bird feeders, don't overfill them. Store birdseed in sealed containers.

2. Remove standing water. Dishes under your plants provide easy access to water for rodents. And dampness attracts bugs. Remove water-damaged material from your home and fix any leaky plumbing. Mosquitoes need less than one inch of water to breed, so don't let rainwater accumulate in toys, buckets or other items in your yard.

3. Remove trash and storage boxes that look like inviting homes. Don't store newspapers or piles of clothing. Remember that wood, even fresh wood, can attract termites and ants. So don't allow old wood to accumulate in your yard.

4. Search the outside of your house for tiny holes that could allow bugs or rodents to enter your home. Caulk any holes and cracks, and cover exposed drains and vents with screen. Keep mulch and vegetation a minimum of 18 inches from the foundation of your home. Trim tree branches away from your house so rodents can't use them to gain access to the roof and attic.
